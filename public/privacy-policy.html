Privacy Policy for Wallet+

Last Updated: November 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Wallet+ ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Wallet+ handles your information.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Wallet+ is a completely offline application that stores all your financial data locally on your device.

Information You Provide

  • Financial transactions and records
  • Budget information
  • Financial goals and preferences
  • Account details (stored only on your device)

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored exclusively on your device using browser local storage. Your data is:

  • Device-Only Storage - All data stored in browser localStorage
  • Local Only - Never transmitted to any servers
  • Private - Only accessible on your device and browser
  • Portable - You can export and backup your data anytime

Security Note: Since this is an offline app with no user authentication, your data security depends on your device security. We recommend using device-level security features (screen lock, biometrics) to protect your financial information.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • IP addresses
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Any third-party tracking data

5. Third-Party Services

Wallet+ does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools. We do not share your data with anyone because we never have access to it.

6. Cookies

Wallet+ does not use cookies for tracking. We only use local storage for saving your financial data on your device.

7. Data Security

We implement security best practices:

  • Input validation and sanitization to prevent malicious data
  • No server-side storage or transmission
  • Browser's built-in localStorage security
  • Open-source codebase for transparency

Important: Your data's security ultimately depends on your device security. We strongly recommend:

  • Using a secure device password/PIN
  • Keeping your device and browser updated
  • Not sharing your device with untrusted users
  • Regularly backing up your data

8. Children's Privacy

Wallet+ does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is transmitted or collected, COPPA requirements are not applicable.

9. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: You can access all your data within the app
  • Export: Export your data anytime using the backup feature
  • Delete: Clear all data using the reset function
  • Control: Complete ownership of your financial information

10. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it. We have no access to your data and cannot delete it for you.

11. International Users

Wallet+ works entirely offline. Your data never leaves your device, so international data transfer laws do not apply.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the app's feedback feature.

14. Consent

By using Wallet+, you acknowledge that you have read and understand this Privacy Policy.
